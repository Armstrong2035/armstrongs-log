# Applied AI: Consultative Commerce & The Emerson Project

## Roadmap Overview
1. **The Vision:** An introduction to the shift from static e-commerce to consultative commerce.
2. **Emerson Research:** Exploring the "Knowledge Core" and grounding AI in objective ground truth.
3. **The Primitives:** A deep dive into the six modular blocks powering the engine.
4. **ML Application:** Implementing unsupervised clustering and regression in production.
5. **Multi-Tenant Infrastructure:** Scaling the architecture to support diverse merchant domains.
