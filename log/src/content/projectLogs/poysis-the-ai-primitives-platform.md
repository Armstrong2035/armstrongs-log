Poysis is an AI primitives platform that lets domain experts build intelligent, customer-facing co-pilots without writing code.

The insight behind Poysis is simple: every AI product that helps humans make better decisions is built from the same seven building blocks. We call them primitives. The Empath understands what someone needs beneath what they said. The Indexer creates a semantic layer over any catalog or knowledge base. The Doctor grounds recommendations in real data. The Librarian remembers context across sessions. The Strategist navigates constraints. The Profiler builds an evolving model of who the user is. The Researcher gathers real-time intelligence to fill gaps.

Today those primitives are assembled by engineers. Poysis makes them composable by anyone.

A hair care brand composes Empath plus Indexer plus Doctor and gets a product concierge that understands curl patterns and porosity. A speaker manufacturer adds Researcher and gets a maintenance guide that answers questions no manual ever anticipated. A fashion retailer adds Profiler and Librarian and gets a stylist that remembers you.

The same seven primitives. Infinite configurations. Any domain where a human needs a knowledgeable guide to make a confident decision.

Product Scout — our Shopify search product — is primitive one deployed in e-commerce. It is the proof that the model works. Poysis is what happens when you make that composability available to everyone.

We are not building another AI assistant. We are building the infrastructure layer that makes domain-specific AI assistants possible — for builders who understand their domain deeply but shouldn't need to understand transformer architecture to deploy intelligence into their products.

The bet is simple: no single player can build everything for every domain. The winners will be the domain experts who can move fast with the right infrastructure behind them. Poysis is that infrastructure.
